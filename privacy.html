Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026 (v4)  ·  Developer: Anastasios Panagou, Switzerland  ·  Platforms: iOS & Android  ·  hello@tekneio.com

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SimpleFit Tracker (“the app”, “we”) processes your personal data. SimpleFit Tracker is available for iOS and Android and follows a strict data minimization principle: the app does not operate its own servers, uses no advertising, and integrates no analytics or tracking SDKs. All data either stays on your device or, on iOS, is stored in your private iCloud account, which only you can access. On Android, data stays exclusively on your device unless you choose to export it.

This policy is intended to meet the requirements of the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (DSG/revFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Controller (data controller) Anastasios Panagou, Switzerland
hello@tekneio.com

2. Data We Process

2.1 Workout & Activity Logs

What: Your workout entries (date, activity type, duration, intensity, notes) and any custom sport types or categories you create.

Where stored:

  • iOS: Exclusively in your private iCloud CloudKit database. Data is encrypted by Apple and is accessible only to you – the developer has no access.
  • Android: Exclusively on your device in the app’s private storage (Room database). No cloud sync. You can export your data at any time via Settings > Data.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Retention: Until you delete the data in the app, uninstall the app, or remove it from your iCloud account (iOS). You fully control retention.

2.2 Health Data (Apple Health / Health Connect) Sensitive

What: With your explicit permission, the app integrates with the platform health service:

  • iOS — Apple HealthKit:
    • Read: Workouts (HKWorkoutType), Active Energy Burned, Step Count – to import your existing Apple Health workouts and activity.
    • Write: Workouts (HKWorkoutType) – new workouts can optionally be written back to Apple Health.
  • Android — Health Connect:
    • Read: Exercise Sessions – to import existing workouts from Health Connect into SimpleFit Tracker. Writing is currently not implemented on Android.

Where stored: Health data is exchanged directly with Apple Health (iOS) or Health Connect (Android) on your device. Data read from either source is never stored on any server, never transmitted to the developer, and never shared with any third party. Workouts written to Apple Health are stored and managed by Apple Health and are subject to Apple’s privacy policy.

Sensitive data: Health data is a special category of personal data under Art. 9 GDPR and particularly sensitive personal data under Swiss law. It is processed solely on the basis of your explicit consent (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR), which you grant when you enable Health integration in the app or in your platform’s health settings.

Withdraw consent:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > SimpleFit Tracker.
  • Android: Open the Health Connect app > App permissions > SimpleFit Tracker > revoke access.

Revoking access stops both reading and (on iOS) writing. Workouts already written to Apple Health can be deleted in the Apple Health app.

SimpleFit Tracker does not use health data for advertising purposes and does not sell it to third parties or data brokers.

2.3 App Settings & Preferences

What: Your optional name, weekly goal, appearance settings, notification preferences, accent color, and custom background selection.

Where stored: Locally on your device only. iOS uses UserDefaults; Android uses Jetpack DataStore. This data is never transmitted or synced by the app to any server.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Retention: Until you uninstall the app or reset the settings.

2.4 Widget Data

What: Active days this week, your current streak, and the selected accent colour – used to power the home screen widget.

Where stored: Locally on your device. iOS uses an app group shared container between the app and the widget extension; Android uses the same DataStore the main app reads from, accessed by the Glance widget worker. This data is never transmitted.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

2.5 In‑App Purchase

SimpleFit Pro is sold exclusively through the platform store:

  • iOS: Apple’s App Store via the StoreKit 2 framework.
  • Android: Google Play via the Google Play Billing Library.

Purchase processing and receipt verification are handled entirely by Apple or Google on your device and through their servers. The app does not use any third-party purchase verification service. The developer has no access to payment details such as credit card numbers.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

For information about how the platforms process transaction data, see Apple’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Privacy Policy.

2.6 Local Notifications

Local notifications (for example, daily reminders) are scheduled and delivered entirely by your operating system (iOS or Android). No separate notification server is used and no notification data leaves your device.

2.7 Trial Period Tracking

What: The timestamp of your first app launch, used to determine whether your 7-day free trial is still active.

Where stored: Locally in UserDefaults (iOS) or DataStore (Android). On iOS, it is also mirrored to NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore (iCloud Key-Value Store) to prevent trial resets through reinstall. No personal identification is associated with this timestamp.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

3. What We Do NOT Collect

SimpleFit Tracker does not:

  • Collect advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Use analytics or crash‑reporting SDKs
  • Sell or share your data for marketing purposes
  • Perform automated profiling or automated individual decision‑making
  • Operate its own backend server for user data

4. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, and subject to legal conditions, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to the personal data that the app processes about you
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure of data, where there is no legal reason to keep it
  • Restriction of certain processing activities
  • Data portability, where applicable
  • Objection to certain processing
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal

To exercise these rights, contact: hello@tekneio.com. A response will usually be provided within 30 days.

How to delete your data

  • Workout data (iOS): Delete entries directly in the app, or go to Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Account Storage > SimpleFit Tracker.
  • Workout data (Android): Delete entries directly in the app, or uninstall the app to remove all local data. You can also export the data first via Settings > Data > Export JSON.
  • Health access (iOS): Revoke in Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > SimpleFit Tracker. Workouts already written to Apple Health can be deleted in the Apple Health app.
  • Health access (Android): Open the Health Connect app > App permissions > SimpleFit Tracker > revoke.
  • Purchase data: In-app purchases are processed by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). Refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy or Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Any other request: Contact hello@tekneio.com.

6. Security

iCloud data (iOS) is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. Local data is protected by iOS sandboxing and device encryption on Apple devices, and by Android’s per-app storage isolation on Android devices. The app does not use unencrypted storage and does not operate its own servers.

7. Children

SimpleFit Tracker has the following content ratings: 9+ on the App Store (Apple content rating), PEGI 3 on Google Play (IARC content rating). On Google Play, the app is marketed to users aged 13 and older. The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, contact hello@tekneio.com.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the current version. For material changes, you will be informed within the app. Any new processing that requires consent will be explicitly requested in the app.
