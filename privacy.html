Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026 (v3)  ·  Developer: Anastasios Panagou, Switzerland  ·  hello@tekneio.com

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SimpleFit Tracker (“the app”, “we”) processes your personal data. SimpleFit Tracker follows a strict data minimization principle: the app does not operate its own servers, uses no advertising, and integrates no analytics or tracking SDKs. All data either stays on your device or is stored in your private iCloud account, which only you can access.

This policy is intended to meet the requirements of the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (DSG/revFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Controller (data controller) Anastasios Panagou, Switzerland
hello@tekneio.com

2. Data We Process

2.1 Workout & Activity Logs

What: Your workout entries (date, activity type, duration, intensity, notes) and any custom sport types or categories you create.

Where stored: Exclusively in your private iCloud CloudKit database. This data is encrypted by Apple and is accessible only to you – the developer has no access.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Retention: Until you delete the data in the app or remove it from your iCloud account. You fully control retention.

2.2 Apple HealthKit Data Sensitive

What: With your explicit permission, the app requests the following access to Apple Health:

  • Read: Workouts (HKWorkoutType), Active Energy Burned (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierActiveEnergyBurned), Step Count (HKQuantityTypeIdentifierStepCount) – to import your existing Apple Health workouts and activity into SimpleFit Tracker.
  • Write: Workouts (HKWorkoutType) – when you log a new workout in SimpleFit Tracker, it can optionally be written back to Apple Health so it shows up there as well.

Where stored: HealthKit data is exchanged directly with Apple Health on your device. Data read from Apple Health is never stored on any server, never transmitted to the developer, and never shared with any third party. Workouts written to Apple Health are stored and managed by Apple Health and are subject to Apple’s privacy policy.

Sensitive data: Health data is a special category of personal data under Art. 9 GDPR and particularly sensitive personal data under Swiss law. It is processed solely on the basis of your explicit consent (for example Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR), which you grant when you enable Health access in the app or in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.

Withdraw consent: You can withdraw Health access at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > SimpleFit Tracker. Revoking access stops both reading and writing. Workouts already written to Apple Health can be deleted in the Apple Health app.

SimpleFit Tracker does not use HealthKit data for advertising purposes and does not sell HealthKit data to third parties or data brokers.

2.3 App Settings & Preferences

What: Your optional name, weekly goal, appearance settings, and notification preferences.

Where stored: Locally on your device only (UserDefaults). This data is never transmitted or synced by the app.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Retention: Until you uninstall the app or reset the settings.

2.4 Widget Data

What: Active days this week, your current streak, and the selected accent colour – used to power the home screen widget.

Where stored: Locally in an app group shared container between the app and the widget extension. This data is never transmitted.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

2.5 In‑App Purchase (Apple StoreKit)

SimpleFit Pro is sold exclusively through Apple’s App Store using Apple’s StoreKit framework. Purchase processing and receipt verification are handled entirely by Apple on your device and through Apple’s servers. The app does not use any third-party purchase verification service. The developer has no access to payment details such as credit card numbers.

Legal basis (where GDPR applies): Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

For information about how Apple processes transaction data, see Apple’s Privacy Policy.

2.6 Local Notifications

Local notifications (for example, reminders) are handled entirely by iOS. No separate notification server is used and no notification data leaves your device.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

SimpleFit Tracker does not:

  • Collect advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Use analytics or crash‑reporting SDKs
  • Sell or share your data for marketing purposes
  • Perform automated profiling or automated individual decision‑making
  • Operate its own backend server for user data

4. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, and subject to legal conditions, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to the personal data that the app processes about you
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure of data, where there is no legal reason to keep it
  • Restriction of certain processing activities
  • Data portability, where applicable
  • Objection to certain processing
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal

To exercise these rights, contact: hello@tekneio.com. A response will usually be provided within 30 days.

How to delete your data

  • Workout data: Delete entries directly in the app, or go to iOS Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Account Storage > SimpleFit Tracker.
  • HealthKit access and data: Revoke Health access in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > SimpleFit Tracker. Workouts already written to Apple Health can be deleted in the Apple Health app.
  • Purchase data: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. For questions about Apple’s processing of transaction data, refer to Apple’s Privacy Policy.
  • Any other request: Contact hello@tekneio.com.

5. Right to Lodge a Complaint

Switzerland Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC)
www.edoeb.admin.ch

6. Security

iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple. Local data is protected by iOS sandboxing and device encryption. The app does not use unencrypted storage and does not operate its own servers.

7. Children

SimpleFit Tracker is rated 9+ on the App Store and is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, contact hello@tekneio.com.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top reflects the current version. For material changes, you will be informed within the app. Any new processing that requires consent will be explicitly requested in the app.

2.2 Apple HealthKit‑Daten Sensibel

Was: Mit Ihrer ausdrücklichen Genehmigung fordern wir folgenden Zugriff auf Apple Health an:

  • Lesen: Trainingseinheiten (HKWorkoutType), aktiv verbrannte Kalorien (activeEnergyBurned), Schrittanzahl (stepCount) – um bestehende Apple‑Health‑Trainings in SimpleFit Tracker zu importieren.

Wo gespeichert: HealthKit‑Daten werden direkt mit Apple Health auf Ihrem Gerät ausgetauscht. Von Apple Health gelesene Daten werden weder auf Servern gespeichert noch übermittelt oder mit Dritten geteilt. In Apple Health geschriebene Trainings werden von Apple Health verwaltet und unterliegen Apples Datenschutzrichtlinien.

Sensible Daten: Gesundheitsdaten sind besonders schützenswerte Personendaten gemäss Art. 9 DSGVO und nach Schweizer Datenschutzrecht. Wir bearbeiten sie ausschliesslich auf Basis Ihrer ausdrücklichen Einwilligung (z.B. Art. 9 Abs. 2 lit. a DSGVO), die Sie durch Aktivierung des Gesundheitszugriffs in der App oder unter iOS‑Einstellungen > Datenschutz & Sicherheit > Gesundheit erteilen.

Einwilligung widerrufen: Sie können den Zugriff jederzeit unter iOS‑Einstellungen > Datenschutz & Sicherheit > Gesundheit > SimpleFit Tracker widerrufen. Das Widerrufen stoppt sowohl Lesen als auch Schreiben. Bereits in Apple Health geschriebene Trainings können in der Apple‑Health‑App gelöscht werden.

HealthKit‑Daten werden nicht für Werbezwecke verwendet und nicht an Dritte oder Datenhändler weitergegeben.
